Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

PT HAMMEREX PRODUCTS INDONESIA ("we", "us", "our") operates the INDOO platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use our services, including bike rides, car rides, food delivery, marketplace, and deals.

1. Data We Collect

Data TypeDetails
IdentityFull name, email address, phone number
LocationGPS coordinates (real-time and historical)
Photos & DocumentsProfile photos, identity documents (KTP, SIM), payment proof screenshots
PaymentPayment method details, transaction history, wallet balance
DeviceDevice model, OS version, unique device identifiers, IP address

2. Why We Collect Your Data

  • Service delivery: To connect you with drivers, restaurants, and marketplace sellers for rides, food delivery, and purchases.
  • Account verification: To confirm your identity and prevent fraud using government-issued documents.
  • Safety: To maintain platform safety, resolve disputes, and comply with regulatory requirements.
  • Improvements: To analyze usage patterns and improve the INDOO platform experience.
  • Communications: To send order updates, receipts, and service announcements.

3. How We Store Your Data

  • Supabase: Primary database for user accounts and transactional data, with encryption at rest and in transit.
  • Firebase: Authentication tokens, push notifications, and real-time data synchronization.
  • ImageKit: Photo storage and delivery for profile images and uploaded documents.

All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (TLS 1.2+ in transit, AES-256 at rest).

4. Third-Party Services

We share data with the following third-party providers strictly as necessary to operate our services:

  • Supabase — Database and authentication
  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, notifications, analytics
  • Stripe — Payment processing
  • Mapbox — Mapping and route calculation
  • ImageKit — Image storage and optimization
  • Google Maps — Location services and geocoding

Each third party is bound by their own privacy policies and data processing agreements.

5. Location Data

We use your location data to:

  • Match you with nearby drivers and service providers
  • Track deliveries in real-time for both customers and restaurants
  • Display nearby services, deals, and marketplace listings
  • Calculate accurate fares and delivery fees

Background location is collected only during active deliveries or rides. You can disable background location access at any time through your device settings, though this may affect service functionality during active orders.

6. Camera and Photos

Camera access is requested for the following purposes:

  • Uploading profile photos
  • Document verification (KTP, SIM) for driver and merchant onboarding
  • Capturing payment proof screenshots for dispute resolution

Camera access is only activated when you explicitly initiate a photo capture or upload within the app.

7. Cookies and Local Storage

We use cookies and local storage for:

  • Session management and authentication persistence
  • Remembering your preferences (language, location, theme)
  • Analytics to understand how users interact with our platform

8. Data Retention

  • Active account data is retained for as long as your account exists and remains active.
  • Transaction records are retained for a minimum of 5 years to comply with Indonesian tax and financial regulations.
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days, in accordance with GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure) and Indonesian PDP law.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — Request a copy of all personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction — Update or correct inaccurate personal data.
  • Deletion — Request permanent deletion of your account and associated data.
  • Data Export — Download your data in a portable, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@indoo.id.

10. Children's Privacy

INDOO is not designed for or directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will promptly delete that information.

11. Applicable Law

This Privacy Policy is governed by Indonesian law, including:

  • UU No. 27 Tahun 2022 — Undang-Undang Perlindungan Data Pribadi (Personal Data Protection Law / PDP)
  • UU No. 11 Tahun 2008 — Undang-Undang Informasi dan Transaksi Elektronik (Electronic Information and Transactions Law / ITE)

We also respect the principles of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in applicable jurisdictions.
