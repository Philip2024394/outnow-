Payment Information & Disclaimer

How payments work on the INDOO platform

INDOO is a physical services platform. All transactions are for physical services (transportation, food delivery, marketplace goods). No digital goods are sold through this application.

Payment Model

INDOO operates exclusively on a Cash on Delivery (COD) payment model for all customer-facing transactions. No online payments are processed through the application for end-user services.

How It Works — Food Delivery

  • Customer places an order through the INDOO app
  • Driver collects the food from the restaurant/vendor and pays upfront
  • Customer pays the driver in cash upon delivery
  • No online payment is processed at any stage

How It Works — Bike & Car Rides

  • Customer books a ride through the INDOO app
  • Driver completes the journey
  • Customer pays the driver in cash at the destination
  • Fare is calculated by the platform as a marketplace pricing tool

Driver & Restaurant Wallet

The INDOO wallet is a physical service administration tool used exclusively for:

  • Tracking platform service fees (10% commission on completed physical services)
  • Maintaining minimum operational balances for service providers
  • Managing cash float for COD food delivery operations

The wallet does not store, process, or transfer money digitally for consumer purchases. It is an administrative ledger for tracking service provider obligations to the platform.

Wallet Top-Up Process

Service providers (drivers and restaurants) top up their administrative wallet via direct bank transfer to PT HAMMEREX PRODUCTS INDONESIA's designated bank account. This is a business-to-business transaction between the platform and its independent service providers.

  • Transfer is made via standard bank transfer (BCA or equivalent)
  • Proof of transfer (screenshot) is submitted through the app
  • Balance is verified manually by the INDOO operations team
  • No automated online payment processing is involved

What INDOO Does NOT Do

  • Does not process consumer payments online
  • Does not sell digital goods, subscriptions, or virtual items
  • Does not act as a payment processor or digital wallet for consumers
  • Does not charge consumers through the application
  • Does not offer in-app purchases of any kind

Platform Commission

INDOO charges a 10% platform service fee to independent service providers (drivers and restaurants) for use of the marketplace technology. This fee is:

  • A business-to-business service charge
  • Paid via bank transfer (not through the app)
  • For the use of the digital marketplace platform only
  • Not a consumer-facing charge

Account Administration

Deactivated accounts require a reactivation fee of Rp 100,000 payable via bank transfer to PT HAMMEREX PRODUCTS INDONESIA. This is an administrative fee between business partners, not a consumer charge.

Marketplace Goods

Physical goods sold through the INDOO marketplace are transactions between independent buyers and sellers. INDOO acts solely as a digital marketplace platform facilitating connections. All payments for physical goods are handled directly between buyer and seller.

Compliance Statement

This payment model is fully compliant with Google Play Store policies as all transactions are for physical services and physical goods, which are exempt from Google Play's billing system requirements as stated in Google Play's Payments Policy (Section 3).
