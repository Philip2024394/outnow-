Refund Policy

Last updated: April 2026

PT HAMMEREX PRODUCTS INDONESIA ("we", "us", "our") is committed to fair refund practices for all INDOO users. This policy outlines the conditions under which refunds are available for each service category.

1. Food Delivery Refunds

Eligible for Refund

  • Order was not delivered
  • Wrong items were delivered
  • Food quality issues (damaged, spoiled, or significantly different from description)
  • Missing items from the order

Reporting window: You must report the issue within 2 hours of the expected delivery time.

Photo evidence may be required to process food delivery refund claims.

2. Ride Refunds (Bike & Car)

Eligible for Refund

  • Driver no-show (driver did not arrive at pickup location)
  • Safety incident during the ride
  • Significant route deviation resulting in overcharge
  • Ride cancelled by driver after acceptance

Reporting window: You must report the issue within 24 hours of the ride.

3. Marketplace Refunds

Eligible for Return & Refund

  • Item not as described in the listing
  • Item arrived damaged or defective
  • Wrong item received
  • Item not received within the estimated delivery window

Return window: You may initiate a return within 7 days of receiving the item.

The seller is responsible for return shipping costs when the return is due to seller error or misrepresentation. Items must be returned in their original condition.

4. Non-Refundable Items

  • Wallet top-ups: Non-refundable once the top-up has been verified and credited to your wallet. Wallet balance can be used for any INDOO service.
  • Driver/restaurant commissions: Platform commissions charged to service providers are non-refundable.
  • Completed services: Services that were delivered as described and without issue are not eligible for refund.
  • Promotional credits: Bonus credits or promotional balances cannot be refunded or converted to cash.

5. Refund Process

  1. Submit a request — Use the "Contact Us" or "Help" section in the INDOO app to submit your refund request with details and any supporting evidence (photos, screenshots).
  2. Review — Our support team will review your request within 72 hours of submission.
  3. Resolution — You will be notified of the outcome via the app and email.
  4. Refund issued — Approved refunds are processed within 7 business days of approval.

6. Refund Method

Refunds are issued through one of the following methods:

  • Original payment method: If you paid by card or bank transfer, the refund will be returned to the same payment method.
  • INDOO Wallet credit: In some cases, refunds may be issued as wallet credit for faster processing. You will be given the option to choose where applicable.

Processing times may vary depending on your bank or payment provider. Card refunds typically take 5-10 business days to appear on your statement.

7. Disputes

If you disagree with a refund decision, you may escalate the dispute by replying to the resolution notification. Escalated disputes are reviewed by a senior support representative and resolved within 14 days.
